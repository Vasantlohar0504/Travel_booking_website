Jaipur Palace, Rajasthan

How to Go There: Fly to Jaipur (Sanganer Airport) or take a train from Delhi or Mumbai.

Cost to Travel: Approx. ₹3,000 - ₹12,000 (Flight/Train + Local transport)

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Popular Activities: Visit Hawa Mahal, Amer Fort, explore the City Palace, and shop for handicrafts in the bazaars.

Travel Tips: Wear comfortable shoes for exploring forts and palaces. Don’t miss out on the local Rajasthani cuisine.

Review: "A royal experience full of history and culture. Jaipur is a perfect blend of tradition and magnificence!"
