Kerala Backwaters

How to Go There: Fly to Kochi or Trivandrum, and take a boat ride from Alappuzha or Kumarakom.

Cost to Travel: Approx. ₹7,000 - ₹20,000 (Flight + Boat Ride + Local transport)

Best Time to Visit: November to March

Popular Activities: Houseboat ride, visit Vembanad Lake, explore the backwaters.

Travel Tips: Pack light and comfortable clothes. Don’t forget to bring a camera to capture the serene beauty.

Review: "A peaceful and scenic experience, surrounded by lush greenery and tranquil waters!"
