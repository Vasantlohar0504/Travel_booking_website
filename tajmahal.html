Taj Mahal, Agra

How to Go There: Fly to Agra or take a train from Delhi.

Cost to Travel: Approx. ₹5,000 - ₹15,000 (Flight/Train + Local transport)

Best Time to Visit: October to March

Popular Activities: Visit the Taj Mahal, explore Agra Fort, take a boat ride on the Yamuna River.

Travel Tips: Wear comfortable shoes, as there is a lot of walking. Carry sunscreen and water.

Review: "A magnificent monument, well maintained, and a must-see in India!"
