Goa Beaches

How to Go There: Fly to Goa (Dabolim Airport) or take a train from Mumbai or Bangalore.

Cost to Travel: Approx. ₹5,000 - ₹10,000 (Flight/Train + Local transport)

Best Time to Visit: November to February

Popular Activities: Beach parties, water sports (parasailing, jet skiing), visit Fort Aguada, explore the vibrant markets.

Travel Tips: Carry sunscreen, a hat, and beachwear. Make sure to stay hydrated and enjoy the local seafood.

Review: "A perfect blend of sun, sand, and sea. Whether you’re looking for relaxation or adventure, Goa has it all!"
